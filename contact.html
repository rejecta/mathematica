Contacting Rejecta Mathematica

Rejecta Mathematica is published by Rejecta Publications, a non-profit corporation based in Texas. The editors can be reached via e-mail at editors@rejecta.org.

  • To submit a paper, please consult the author guidelines.
  • To submit a response or criticism of a paper, please consult the instructions for submitting a correspondence.
  • For other information about Rejecta Mathematica, please contact the editors.
  • Other Rejecta journals may follow in disciplines outside themathematical sciences. Please contact us if you are interested instarting your own Rejecta franchise.
